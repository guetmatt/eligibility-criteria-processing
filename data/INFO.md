Training data for NER and RE models.
Chia dataset by Kury et al. (2020).

- chia_without_scope = raw Chia dataset without Scope annotations
- chia_without_scope_parsedNER_v1 = parsed Chia dataset for NER
- chia_without_scope_parsedRE_noDownsampling = parsed Chia dataset for RE, no downsampling applied
- chia_without_scope_parsedRE_globalAndTrainDownsampling = parsed Chia dataset for RE, downsampling applied in two steps: initial downsampling of full dataset, followed by downsampling of Train split specifically
